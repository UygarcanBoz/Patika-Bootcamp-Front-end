...Chocolate Cubes...

Chocolate and pistachio-flavored bite-sized treats... Chocolate cubes, made with only 4 ingredients, are served on colorful papers or skewered with toothpicks. Chocolate cubes, particularly featured on crowded tables, can also be served at special events like birthdays.

Recipe: Uygarcan Boz

Serving Size : 4 servings   Preparation Time : 30 minutes   Cooking Time : 10 minutes

For the chocolate cubes recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 350 grams of dark chocolate
  • 150 milliliters of cream
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 150 grams of pistachios (Almonds or hazelnuts can also be used)

How to Make Chocolate Cubes:

  1. Melt dark chocolate using a double boiler.
  2. Once melted, add cream and butter to the chocolate and mix thoroughly, then transfer the mixture onto a countertop.
  3. Lightly crush the pistachios in a mortar and pestle, breaking them into small pieces.
  4. Add the crushed pistachios to the chocolate mixture and mix thoroughly.
  5. Pour the prepared chocolate mixture into either a pre-prepared square aluminum tray, or if unavailable, onto a small tray lined with parchment paper, ensuring it reaches the edges.
  6. Place the tray in the refrigerator and let it set for two hours.
  7. Remove the chocolate from the mold and cut it into square pieces.
